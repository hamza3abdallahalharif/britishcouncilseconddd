Addressed to
The British Council
Cairo, Egypt — 2026
Dear British Council,

It is with immense pride and heartfelt sincerity that we present to you this project — the result of extraordinary dedication, sleepless nights, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

This was not simply an academic exercise. It was a journey of growth, collaboration, and genuine passion for what we believe in. We challenged ourselves at every turn, pushed beyond our comfort zones, and refused to settle for anything less than our very best.

We placed our full hearts and minds into every detail — from the first brainstorming session to the final touch — because we believe that the work we put out into the world should reflect who we truly are.

May this work inspire, impress, and above all — amaze you. We are deeply honoured to share it with an institution of your distinction, and we are grateful for the platform and opportunity you have provided us.

With great respect and gratitude,

The Project Team
